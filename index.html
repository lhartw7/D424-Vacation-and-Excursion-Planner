Vacation and Excursion Planner

The VEP app will have a simple and efficient interface. When entering the app customers of WWV will be able to add a new vacation and enter details such as date, name, and lodging. While creating a vacation or later on, the customer will be able to add excursions to their vacation. Excursion details include date and the name of the excursion. Customers can receive notifications of their vacations and excursions. Customers will also be able to quickly find information about their vacation packages using a built in search function. Finally, if customers wish to, they can generate a report of their vacations and excursions to keep separately or print. This will help resolve the first need of WWV, which is to reduce the extra volume of calls that the customer service team is receiving. This is because customers are calling to receive information about their vacation package and now can enter it into the app instead. The app will also help resolve the second issue that WWV is having. That is that customers often complain that they miss excursions because of losing track or forgetting. The app will have the option to notify the customers so they don’t lose track.


How to use?

Once the app is launched, press the enter button to open the VEP.

Here you can create a new vacation with the + at the bottom right of the screen or select an existing vacation.

This opens the vacation details where you can view or update them. You will also see the vacation excursions listed below the details (if any exist).

Pressing the 3 dots at the top right will give you 4 options. You can Save, Delete, Alert, or Share the vacation.

Pressing the + at the bottom right of the Vacation Details page will let you add an excursion.

On the Excursion Details page you can see the details of the excursion and update them.

Pressing the three dots at the top right of the Excursion Details page lets you Save, Delete, or Alert.
